## Featured
**National Farmworker Awareness Week**
This National Farmworkers Awareness Week, we are thrilled to partner with Student Action with Farmworkers, the student-led nonprofit responsible for organizing this nationwide event for the past 28 years. We are grateful for the chance to chat with SAF Executive Director Sylvia Zapata, exploring the organization’s history, impact, and hopes for the future.
